Frequently Asked Questions

What image formats are supported?

We support JPEG, PNG, and WebP formats for both input and output.

Is there a file size limit?

No, but very large images may take longer to process depending on your device.

How much can I reduce my image size?

Reduction depends on the original image quality and format. Typically you can reduce size by 50-80% with minimal quality loss.
